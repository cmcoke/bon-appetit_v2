Located on the border of NoHo and the East Village, Bon Appétit captures the warmth and comfort of a Roman trattoria, with a menu that reflects the rich cultures of both Rome and New York. Executive Chef Michael White draws inspiration from the Roman cooking tradition to create soulful food that celebrates the restaurant’s ongoing relationship with its local family of farmers and suppliers. Bon Appétit attracts those who don't chase luxury, but who live it-by elevating life's moments, indulging their senses, and celebrating every day with the best food, service, and surroundings in the world.

What Food Critics Say About Us

Unpretentious but with a pure NY vibe, Bon Appétit is a go-to for simple yet refined Italian-Med fare especially their appetizers and entrées – in a homey NoHo setup with communal seats and somewhat pricey tabs aside, it’s comfortable for just about every occasion.

The New York Times

The best Italian in Noho? Not that there’s a ton of competition, but that would be Bon Appétit. This restaurant has been around for over two decades, and it’s a classic date-night move. It’s cozy and quaint (the space used to be an antique store), and the pastas won’t let you down. It isn’t cheap, but it also isn’t 50th-birthday or just-won-a-Grammy expensive.

Condé Nast Traveler

Bon Appétit hits the nail on the head with there wonderfully creative dishes that burst with bold flavors; a Downtown sceney crowd keeps the industrial digs feeling cool, with a fun vibe along with the full menu.

Vogue

Time after time, Bon Appétit lives up to its billing with exquisite cuisines that dazzles your palate served with balletlike precision in a serene, luxurious setting that requires a jacket; it’ll cost a king’s ransom to settle the tab, but those willing to splurge can count on an unforgettable meal.

Time Out

This intimate NoHo fave delivers value for the quality with delicious, trattoria-style Tuscan fare set down by a warm staff; as it’s consistently packed (for good reason), followers only wish for a tad more elbow room.

Fortune
